Guide Classes

MAKE NOTICE THAT ALL CLASS ARE ABLE TO USE SHIELD, CLASSES LIKE PALADIN AND KNIGHT HAVE HER OWN SPECIAL SHIELDS, THAT CAN BE ONLY WEARABLE BY THEM!
You can choose on of the four Starting Class, at Level 50 you can change it to one of the Subclasses, but also change to other Class.
All Classes are Capped to Level 1000.
/c or /class => to choose class
Available Class on the Start are :

The Archer
Main Weapon: Bow or Crossbow
Ability Weapon: Quivers

About the Archer:
- Have very good Projectile Damage
- Good amount of Health
- Not so bad Knockback Resistance
- Is a little bit faster than normal
- Doesn't have so much Max Mana

SUBCLASSES At 50 Level:

The Bard
Main Weapon: Bow or Crossbow
Ability Weapon: Harp (in the Future)

About the Bard:
- Have very good amount of Health
- Move as fast as Archer
- Have the same Knockback Resistance
- Have much more Mana than Archer
- A little bit badder Projectile Damage


The Huntress
Main Weapon: Bow or Crossbow
Ability Weapon: Traps (in the Future)

About the Huntress:
- Have much more Mana than Bard or Archer
- Have little less Max Health than Bard
- Move as fast as Bard or Archer
- Have less Knockback Resistance
- Have bad Projectile Damage


The Wizard
Main Weapon: Staff
Ability Weapon: Spell

About the Wizard:
- Have really much Mana Points
- Have okay Magic Damage but not the best
- Have a little bit of Projectile Damage
- Better Mana Regeneration
- Also better Health Regeneration
- Have less Health
- Very bad Attack Damage
- Very bad Knockback Resistnace

SUBCLASSES At 50 Level:

The Priest
Main Weapon: Staff
Ability Weapon: Tomes

About the Priest:
- Have very buffed Mana Regeneration
- Have very buffed Health Regeneration
- Better Max Health than Wizard
- A little less Max Mana
- Less Projectile Damage and Magic Damage


The Necromancer
Main Weapon: Staff
Ability Weapon: Tomes and Skulls

About the Necromancer:
- Able to Steal Health from Enemies/Players using Ability
- Better Max Health than Wizard
- Bad Health Regeneration
- Less Magic Damage than Wizard
- Less Max Mana
- Very Bad Projectile Damage


The Rogue
Main Weapon: Daggers
Ability Weapon: Cloaks

About the Rogue:
- Able to cast Invisibility for special amount of time
- The best Movement Speed
- The best Attack Speed
- Good Attack Damage but not the best
- Have okay amount of Health
- Badder Knockback Resistance than Tank Classes
- Bad Max Mana

SUBCLASSES At 50 Level:

The Assasin
Main Weapon: Daggers
Ability Weapon: Big Bottles

About the Assasin:
- Potion with special Effects, can buff himself from Big Bottles
- Have Magic Damage
- Better Max mana than Rogue
- Better Knockback Resistance than Rogue
- Badder Movement Speed
- Not so good Attack Damage and Attack Speed like Rogue
- Less Max Health


The Trickster
Main Weapon: Daggers
Ability Weapon: Prisms

About the Trickster:
- Able to Teleport Himself for short distance
- Better Mana Regeneration than Rogue
- Much more Max Mana
- Better Knockback Resistance than Rogue
- Less Movement Speed
- Less Attack Damage and Attack Speed
- Less Max Health


The Warrior
Main Weapon: Swords
Ability Weapon: Helmet's

About the Warrior:
- Able to Get/Craft Special Helmet's with Ability's
- Very Good Knockback Resistance
- Good Max Health
- Better Health Regeneration
- Is little bit faster than normal
- Normal Attack Speed
- Lower Mana Regeneration
- Very Bad Max Mana

SUBCLASSES At 50 Level:

The Knight
Main Weapon: Swords
Ability Weapon: Shields

About the Knight:
- Able to Get/Craft Special Shields with Ability's
- Very Good Knockback Resistance
- Much more Health than Warrior
- Much more Mana than Warrior
- Good Mana Regeneration
- Badder Health Regeneration
- Knight have normal Movement Speed
- Badder Attack Damage and Attack Speed than Warrior


The Paladin
Main Weapon: Swords
Ability Weapon: Shields

About the Paladin:
- Able to Get/Craft Special Shields with Ability's
- Very Good Magic Damage
- Very Good Mana Regeneration
- Good Knockback Resistance
- Best Max Health
- Good Max Mana
- Paladin walks the same as Knight
- Badder Attack Speed
- Slower Health Regeneration
- Very Bad Attack Damage
