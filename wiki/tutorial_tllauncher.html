Tutorial Installing Modpack on Tlauncher
At First you will need to download a Modpack. You have to Options to choose BASE or EXTENDED. Download Here!
Now you will need to Start your Tlauncher and choose "Forge 1.16.5"
After Forge is installed. Open the Folder with the icon on the right side. And Drag and drop all the Files which you Downloaded.
When everything went well, you should see bunch of Mods in mods section. Now just run the Game and have Fun!
