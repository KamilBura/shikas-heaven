Server Rules
General Rules
1. Admins and Moderators won't Kick / Ban for no Reason. Each punishment is justified, so team members do not need to justify it.
2. By using/changing your Prefix, it need to be legible and not sexist, offensive, racist or discriminatory.
3. Any kind of advertising is strictly forbidden and will be punished with PERMANENT BAN. This also applies to sending advertisements to other server members via private messages.
4. Any kind of using Duping/Bug, without calling Moderation at first, will be granted with1 to 5 Day Temporary Ban! + Removed EQ
5. Any kind of Cheating, Kill Aura, Fly, etc. will be Permanently Banned!
6. Using Exploits, without noticing Administrations 1 to 2 Week Temporary Ban! + Removed EQ, if needed
7. Trying to DDos Server, or building Lagging machine will be Permanently Banned!
8. Trying to log-in to not your Account, will be granted with IP-Ban! (if you got IP-Banned, but you were the Owner of Account, Contact Support!)
9. Sharing Password/Account to Third Person will be granted with Permanently Banned!
10. Not knowing Rules will not be counted as an excuse!
Chat Rules
11. Hating other people, through Game Chat will be granted with 1 to 2 Week Temporary Ban!
12. Spamming will not be tolerated 1 to 2 Day Temporary Ban!
13. Linking Links to FSK 18 aka. NSFW content is prohibited. 1 Week Temporary Ban!
Voice Chat Rules
14. Interfering noises (e.g. blowing into the microphone, loud shouting, etc.) are prohibited. Remove Permission to Speak, if done more times!
15. Use of VoiceMod / VoiceChanger are not banned, until it doesn't interfere other Players Remove Permission to Speak, if done more times!
16. Indecent noises, overly loud background noises and echoes should be avoided. In such cases, please use in-game Chat, or other third software like, discord, teamspeak etc.
Playing Rules
17. Breaking other game in any way, will be granted with 1 to 2 Day Ban, for all the People which have something to do with it.
18. Trolling is not banned, if it's not going to much
